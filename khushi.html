Affordable Escapes

Looking for budget friendly international travel?

If you're looking for budget-friendly travel experiences outside of India, there are plenty of options to consider. Exploring new destinations doesn't have to be expensive, and there are many beautiful places where you can enjoy rich cultures, stunning landscapes, and delicious food without overspending. Let's take a look at some affordable destinations that offer a great mix of adventure and relaxation for travelers on a budget:


1. Bali, Indonesia: Known for its beautiful beaches, lush rice terraces, and vibrant culture, Bali offers a mix of relaxation and adventure at affordable prices

2. Vietnam: Explore the bustling streets of Hanoi, cruise through the stunning Halong Bay, and enjoy delicious street food without breaking the bank


3. Thailand: From the bustling markets of Bangkok to the idyllic islands of Phuket and Krabi, Thailand offers a diverse range of experiences at budget-friendly prices.
